# Job Posting Websites (Where Companies Post Jobs)

A comprehensive list of websites where companies post job openings for candidates to find and apply.

---

## Major Job Boards & Search Engines

1. **Indeed** (www.indeed.com) - One of the largest job search engines, aggregating listings from thousands of sources.
2. **LinkedIn Jobs** (www.linkedin.com/jobs) - Professional networking platform with integrated job listings.
3. **Glassdoor** (www.glassdoor.com) - Job listings with company reviews, salaries, and interview insights.
4. **ZipRecruiter** (www.ziprecruiter.com) - Popular job board with AI-powered job matching.
5. **Monster** (www.monster.com) - One of the oldest and most established job boards.
6. **CareerBuilder** (www.careerbuilder.com) - Long-standing job board with resume tools and job alerts.
7. **SimplyHired** (www.simplyhired.com) - Job search engine aggregating listings from across the web.
8. **Google for Jobs** (jobs.google.com) - Google's aggregated job search feature pulling from many sources.
9. **Facebook Jobs** (www.facebook.com/jobs) - Job listings integrated into the Facebook platform.
10. **Dice** (www.dice.com) - Leading job board for technology and IT professionals.

## Niche & Specialized Job Boards

11. **FlexJobs** (www.flexjobs.com) - Curated listings for remote, freelance, and flexible jobs.
12. **Remote.co** (remote.co) - Specialized in remote job opportunities.
13. **Wellfound (formerly AngelList)** (wellfound.com) - Focused on startup and tech jobs.
14. **Upwork** (www.upwork.com) - Freelance and contract job marketplace.
15. **Fiverr** (www.fiverr.com) - Freelance services marketplace.
16. **Snagajob** (www.snagajob.com) - Specializes in hourly and shift-based jobs.
17. **Ladders** (www.ladders.com) - Focused on high-paying professional jobs ($100k+).
18. **Handshake** (joinhandshake.com) - Job platform for college students and recent graduates.
19. **Levels.fyi** (www.levels.fyi) - Job listings with compensation benchmarking data.
20. **Hubstaff Talent** (talent.hubstaff.com) - Free directory for remote talent.
21. **Learn4Good** (www.learn4good.com) - International job listings and educational opportunities.
22. **Guru** (www.guru.com) - Freelance job marketplace.

## Staffing & Recruitment Agencies

23. **Robert Half** (www.roberthalf.com) - Staffing agency with its own job listings.
24. **Adecco** (www.adecco.com) - Global staffing and recruitment agency.
25. **Randstad** (www.randstad.com) - Multinational staffing agency.

## Industry-Specific Job Boards

26. **Idealist** (www.idealist.org) - Nonprofit and social impact jobs.
27. **The Muse** (www.themuse.com) - Career advice and curated job listings.
28. **Crunchboard** (www.crunchboard.com) - Tech and startup jobs.
29. **eFinancialCareers** (www.efinancialcareers.com) - Finance and banking jobs.
30. **Health eCareers** (www.healthecareers.com) - Healthcare industry jobs.
31. **HigherEdJobs** (www.higheredjobs.com) - Academic and higher education positions.
32. **USAJobs** (www.usajobs.gov) - Official job site for U.S. federal government positions.

## Regional & Local Job Boards

33. **Craigslist** (www.craigslist.org) - Local classifieds including job postings.
34. **Mac's List** (www.macslist.org) - Portland, Oregon area jobs.
35. **Job.com** (www.job.com) - Job board with regional and national listings.

## Emerging & Modern Platforms

36. **Getwork** (www.getwork.com) - Job search platform.
37. **Lensa** (www.lensa.com) - AI-powered job matching platform.
38. **Joblist** (www.joblist.com) - Personalized job search aggregator.
39. **4 Day Week** (www.4dayweek.io) - Jobs with 4-day work weeks.
40. **Remote Rocketship** (www.remoterocketship.com) - Remote job listings.
41. **Remote Army** (www.remotearmy.com) - Remote job opportunities.
42. **80,000 Hours** (www.80000hours.org) - High-impact career opportunities.

---

*Note: Many companies also post jobs directly on their own company career pages (e.g., via platforms like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday) alongside these job boards.*
